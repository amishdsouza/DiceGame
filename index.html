Instructions

  • 🎲 Roll the dice: Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.
  • 💰 Accumulate points: The number you roll gets added to your current score unless you roll a 1.
  • ❌ Rolling a 1: If you roll a 1, your current score resets, and it's the other player's turn.
  • 💾 Hold your score: If you want to keep your current score, click the "Hold" button to add it to your total score.
  • 🏆 Winning the game: The first player to reach 100 points wins!
  • 🔄 Start a new game: Click the "New Game" button to reset everything and start fresh.
